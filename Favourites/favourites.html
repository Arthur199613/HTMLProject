  3. Half Life 2

    Half-Life 2 is a 2004 first-person shooter game developed by Valve. It was first published by Valve for Windows through its distribution service Steam. Like the original Half-Life, Half-Life 2 combines shooting, puzzles, and storytelling, and adds features such as vehicles and physics-based gameplay

  5. Quake Live/Quake 3

    Quake Live is a first-person arena shooter video game by id Software. It is an updated version of Quake III Arena that was originally designed as a free-to-play game launched via a web browser plug-in. On September 17, 2014, the game was re-launched as a standalone title on Steam
